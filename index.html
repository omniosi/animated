words Edward Gross

No matter the era, you pretty much know what you're going to get when Superman arrives on the scene. Batman, conversely, is a little more of a mixed bag: is he going to represent the campy Caped Crusader or the deadly serious Dark Knight?

It's a question that has been pondered for decades, and ultimately decided by whoever's pulling his creative strings, but never has it been done more clearly than in the various animated productions. Beginning with Batman: The Animated Series in the Nineties and continueing through different series and animated films, incuding the upcoming Batman: Attack on Arkham, Bruce Wayne has run the gamut from the guy you'd like to have a beer with to the one you'd never want to meet in a dark alley.
